Our Work

With a core lens of diversity and inclusion, we work with businesses, organizations and groups to develop and deliver engaging and impactful workplace education and management solutions.

Our Vision

In our communities and today’s workplace, consistent and continuous education, training and policies that focus on diversity, equity and inclusion awareness are essential for courageous organizations.

Our Approach

With an approach of assess, advise and assist, both Shumba Consulting and the client organization work collaboratively to determine the best fit in terms of service, scope, timeline, desired outcomes, future commitment and expectations.
